Self-assessment is an individual review performed to identify elements that can be improved or exploited to achieve certain predefined goals (healing for examples). In other words, the person analyses himself to improve his perspective about his capabilities and potential. This process is a psychological exercise that individuals in management positions should perform constantly to identify improvement opportunities in all tasks being carried out. Self-assessment can also be the process of looking at oneself in order to assess aspects that are important to one's identity. It is one of the motives that drive self-evaluation, along with self-verification and self-enhancement.
